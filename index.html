Pizza Before Personalities

On the last Wednesday of every month, a group of guys meets at a different pizza place, then visits a meeting.

So you are not surprised, there are some basic rules:

  • No special orders
  • 20% tip
  • Evenly split the check and round up

If you are ACIC, there are other suggestions:

  • No chain restaurants
  • Please confirm they have a seating area (this has been a problem more than once)
  • Soda by the pitcher if available
  • Order garlic bread (with cheese if available) when you arrive before everyone
"Life will take on a new meaning. To watch people recover, to see them help others, to watch loneliness vanish, to see a fellowship grow up about you, to have a host of friends - this is an experience you must not miss... Frequent contact with newcomers and each other is the bright spot of our lives." - Alcoholics Anonymous, Pg. 89

Hope to see you at the next meeting.
